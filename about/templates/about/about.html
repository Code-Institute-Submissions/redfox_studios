Our Studio

Established in 2008, our studio has changed to meet our ever-growing customer base.


Our beginning

Selling at local markets, we developed a loyal and ever growing customer base.

It was a few years onwards we moved into our first studio.

Giving up on our day job, we took the plunge to be full time artists.

We even held our first event at this location.

But space was becoming a bit of an issue with more artworks being produced and we needed to grow.

Close

With new technologies, we began to experiment with digital artworks.

This has formed the basis of our studio.

Then in 2019, we made the move to be a exclusive online.

This way we could create affordable artworks for a wider range of clients.

What's next?

We wish to keep on expanding, growing and creating the artworks our customers love.
